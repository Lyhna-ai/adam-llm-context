# Second Brain Operating System

## Purpose

Adam's Obsidian vault is external memory and a thinking workspace. It should reduce the burden of remembering, filing, and reconstructing context.

## Core Principle

Adam should be able to dump messy material without deciding where it belongs. Codex is responsible for sorting, linking, summarizing, and turning raw material into useful pages when asked.

## Method

The vault follows a lightweight version of CODE and PARA.

CODE:

- Capture: put raw material in `00_inbox/Drop Zone`.
- Organize: connect material to projects, people, topics, and sources.
- Distill: reduce messy material into short summaries, decisions, and reusable ideas.
- Express: turn distilled material into plans, documents, posts, specs, messages, or published notes.

PARA:

- Projects: active outcomes in `20_projects`.
- Areas and doctrine: ongoing rules, values, responsibilities, and preferences in `10_doctrine`.
- Resources: reusable material in `40_reference`.
- Archives and outputs: finished or inactive material in `90_published`.

## Wikipedia Style

Notes should link to other notes generously. Prefer short, durable pages over giant unsorted documents once material has been processed.

Useful page types:

- Concept notes
- Source notes
- Project notes
- Person notes
- Decision notes
